The Beast Floral Brand Channel Strategy Project

Conducted a comprehensive omni-channel analysis for The Beast, a high-end floral and lifestyle brand. The project examines its online/offline channel structure, dual distribution decisions, service gap performance, omni-channel synergy issues, and proposes targeted optimization strategies.

  • Analysis of official website, mini-program, e-commerce & social platforms
  • Evaluation of store experience, logistics & return policies
  • Dual distribution model assessment (in-house logistics + SF Express)
  • Service gap & cost gap analysis using academic frameworks
  • Proposed 6 omni-channel integration strategies
